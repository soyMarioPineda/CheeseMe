
#CheeseMe

This Chrome extension came from a conversation that wasn’t even mine, I just overheard it at a party. Two girls were talking about how they keep track of their boyfriends’ Instagram followers and following lists. It was funny because one of them was literally writing everything down in a notebook or in her phone notes, and then comparing it later to see what changed.

I got home and couldn’t sleep. I kept thinking about it and knew I had to build it.

So here it is. It’s open source.

Basically, you go to someone’s Instagram profile, run the extension, and it generates all the files you need: followers, following, who you follow that doesn’t follow you back, who follows you that you don’t follow, and mutuals. It also generates an Excel file so you can keep versions and track changes over time.

Hopefully one day they find this repo.
